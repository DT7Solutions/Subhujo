About Us

We Help Our Clients

Subhujo Simplifies IT by offering end-to-end solutions for all of your IT challenges. We provide IT consulting , IT staffing , custom software services, data warehouse & mobile resources to some of the largest & most well-known corporations in South Africa and India. Our focus and priority is our talent and our process! We hire the best and optimize that with processes to deliver cost-effective solutions.

Subhujo Technologies is dedicated to developing innovative approaches to assist our clients in taking their businesses to a higher level. Our industry expertise and experience help companies meet and exceed their goals and realize increased growth and profits. We have built a global reputation in the IT community as a great place to work. As a result, we get our choice of the cream of the IT crop. Subhujo offers a complete portfolio of services applicable to a broad spectrum of industries and business models. Our strong domain expertise in a variety of sectors allows us to meet the needs of our clients, no matter how complex. We provide innovative, superior solutions within your time and budget constraints.
